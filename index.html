What will FAGBEMS do?

Topnotch welfarism

We will ensure that the needs of ALL FUNAABITES are met and ensure that no one is left on the sideline or ignored. We will ensure that both Agric students and Non-Agric students are carried along EQUALLY without being bias, and above all, We will ensure that your voice is heard.

Vote FAGBEMS

Quality Service

We would focus more on ensuring basic social amenities that include but not limited to Transportation (major concern), Quality service delivery in academics, Security, Water supply in all hostels, etc.

Vote FAGBEMS

Skill acquisition

We will be more than willing to train our students in various soft, hard, and vocational skills of their choice to enhance their productivity on campus and by extension after graduation, this would thereby enhance their financial Independence
