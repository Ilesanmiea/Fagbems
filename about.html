Meet with FAGEBMS

WHO IS FAGBEMS?

Comr. Fagbemi Temidire Tewogboye, popularly called FAGBEMS, is a Social Entrepreneur, Author, Public Speaker, Project Manager, Politician who served as the Publicity Secretary for the Environmental Management & Toxicology Student Association (EMTSA) during the 18/19 session and was elected as the Electoral Chairman of the same association for 20/21 session and he is Founder of the Pro-Active Youth Empowerment (PAYE); A non-profit organization keen on meeting the needs of the entire youth populace for capacity building, academic, social, and environmental impact, to help the youths transcend beyond the unemployment problem in Nigeria through Leadership training, Entrepreneurship Summit, Skill Acquisition, and Empowerment programs.

EARLY LIFE AND EDUCATION

Fagbemi was born on 24 April 1992 in Lagos Island, Lagos State. He attended Ade-Oshodi Memorial Baptist School, Lagos, between 1999 to 2005 where he completed his primary school education. Then moved to Isale-Eko Grammar School, Lagos, to complete his secondary school education between 2005 to 2010. He was admitted to the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB) in 2017 to study Environmental Management and Toxicology (EMT) after seeking admission for 7 years.

BUSINESS CAREER

Fagbemi has worked in several organizations, brands and companies between 2010 and 2017 after his graduation from secondary school that included but not limited to African fertilizers and chemical limited (AFCL), Ogun. Kleanliving cleaner laundry, Ilorin. Desharp pure water company, Lagos and Morecycle company, Lagos, where he gathered experiences that have helped him in scaling through in his business career today. After he got admitted to FUNAAB in 2017, he was into online affiliate marketing that helped gather funds which made him become independent. Although, it was not all rosy as he encountered challenges trying to invest in different online platforms that brought about a major setback for him in 2018, 2021 and 2022.

We can say that 2019 became his primetime, as in March 2019, Fagbemi authored and published a book titled; The Poor Youth vs. The Rich Youth. A self-help book that illuminates self-discovery, mastery, and development of purpose as a youth and ways to balance academics with business. With over 2,000 copies sold.

He founded the ProActive Youth Empowerment (PAYE) in the same March 2019 and has collaborated with many organizations and brands using different platforms to train youths in digital, vocational, soft, and hard skills, having lots of beneficiaries in FUNAAB and other tertiary institutions, thereby reducing the rate of unemployment in Nigeria.

Fagbemi is a philanthropist who is passionate about helping children, particularly Orphans and indigents. In 2019, he came up with an initiative - Orphans and Vulnerable Children Outreach (OVCO) to help children in underdeveloped & rural communities. For more than 3 years, he has catered for over 2,500 children by supplying writing materials and also placed 1% of the total Children under the PAYE scholarship to foster their education and reduce the rate of poverty.

Fagbemi is a freelance writer utilizing different freelance platforms for work and earns his money legitimately as a student.

POLITICS

Fagbemi served as the former publicity secretary for EMT Student Association during the 2018/2019 session, under the led administration of Comr. Kolawole Joseph.

He was elected as the Electoral Chairman for the 2020/2021 EMT student association election to revitalize the Association with over 200 participants and 197 votes valid. (Something that has never happened before).

He is currently the project manager for COLERM student association working on an environmental summit. A laudable project that is set to wow FUNAAB and ABEOKUTA at large. 

FAGBEMS is a professional resume writer with numerous resumes written, facilitator of many successful projects, a public speaker with an award, a project manager with several projects under his sleeves, and enjoys volunteering.

AWARDS AND HONOR

His passion for problem-solving and leadership capacity building has earned him several certifications, recognitions, and awards that include:

  • The Most creative student award - COLERMSA (2019)
  • The best Public speaker (first runner up) - FUNAAB literary week award (2021)
  • COLERMSA awardee, Most Creative (2022)
  • COLERMSA awardee, Humanitarian of the Year  (2022)
  • COLERMSA awardee, Event Organiser of the Year (2022)
